Our Story

Founded in 2019 at Katni

Jauhari Tyre & Company was founded in 2019 by Nishant Vishwakarma at Katni, Madhya Pradesh. What started as a small tyre retail outlet has grown into one of the region's most trusted tyre dealerships, serving farmers, truckers, and industrial operators across Katni, Satna, and Rewa divisions.

With a commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction, we have partnered with India's leading tyre manufacturers to bring genuine products at competitive prices. Our team of experienced professionals ensures every customer gets the right advice and the best solution for their vehicle.
