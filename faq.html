We are authorised dealers for JK Tyre, MRF, Apollo, CEAT, BKT, TVS, Goodyear, Bridgestone, Michelin, Continental and more. All our tyres are 100% genuine with full manufacturer warranty.

Check your vehicle owner's manual or look at the sidewall of your existing tyre. The size is typically printed as something like "13.6x28" for tractors or "215/65R15" for cars. You can also visit our Size Guide page or call us for assistance.

Tyre remoulding (or retreading) is the process of applying a new tread layer on a worn but structurally sound tyre casing. It is a cost-effective and environmentally friendly option for truck and bus tyres. We offer professional remoulding services at our Maihar workshop.

Tyres should generally be rotated every 5,000-8,000 km or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear, extends tyre life, and improves vehicle handling. We offer free rotation with every tyre purchase.

Recommended tyre pressure varies by vehicle and load. For cars it is typically 30-35 PSI, for tractor rears 18-25 PSI, and for truck tyres 90-110 PSI. Check your vehicle manual or the sticker inside the driver's door. We provide free air and nitrogen filling at our shop.

Tube tyres require an inner tube to hold air, while tubeless tyres seal directly against the rim. Tubeless tyres are safer (slow air loss on puncture), run cooler, and offer better fuel efficiency. Most modern cars and SUVs use tubeless tyres, while tractors and trucks use tube-type or tubeless depending on the rim.
